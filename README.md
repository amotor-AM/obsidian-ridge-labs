# Obsidian Ridge Labs

## Pure privacy. Intelligence on your terms.

Obsidian Ridge Labs is an independent Las Vegas software studio building private AI apps for Apple devices. We create focused tools for the parts of life that should not become raw material for someone else’s cloud: conversations, finances, focus, memory, health, learning, belongings, and relationships.

[Visit the site](https://obsidianridgelabs.com/) · [Read the philosophy](https://obsidianridgelabs.com/philosophy) · [Explore the apps](https://obsidianridgelabs.com/download)

## The Obsidian standard

Powerful software should not require surrendering the context that makes it useful.

- Core intelligence stays on your device whenever supported Apple hardware can do the work.
- Important workflows remain useful offline after setup.
- Connections are specific, visible, and optional where possible.
- Personal data is not an advertising profile.
- Your archive should be searchable, exportable, and under your control.

## Echo Chamber

Echo Chamber is our first released product. It records live or imports existing audio and video, then creates a searchable transcript, notes, summaries, answers, and exports on supported Apple hardware.

It is built for Apple Intelligence and includes a local Bonsai 1.7B fallback for supported devices without Apple Intelligence. Parakeet TDT handles speech recognition, and Echo Chamber’s targeted speech-enhancement pipeline has observed approximately 4.5% word error rate in internal testing. The complete Pro toolkit is available monthly, yearly, or as a $79.99 Lifetime purchase.
